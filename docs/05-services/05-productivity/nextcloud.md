# Service: Nextcloud

### 🧐 What is it?
A private cloud storage and productivity suite.

### 💡 Why is it useful?
- **Auto-Backup:** Syncs your phone's photos to Node C (1TB HDD).
- **File Access:** Securely access your documents from any device.

### ⚖️ Pros & Cons
*   **Pros:** Replaces Google Drive/Photos completely.
*   **Cons:** PHP-based, can be slow; requires regular maintenance for updates.
