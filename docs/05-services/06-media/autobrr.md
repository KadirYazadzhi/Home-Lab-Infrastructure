# Service: Autobrr

### 🧐 What is it?
A download automation tool that monitors IRC for new releases.

### 💡 Why is it useful?
Grabs your favorite TV shows or movies the second they are released.

### ⚖️ Pros & Cons
*   **Pros:** Extremely fast, great filters.
*   **Cons:** Requires knowledge of IRC and RegEx.
